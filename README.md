# 🎨 DoodleWar

**DoodleWar** is a fun, interactive drawing game where your sketches come to life through AI!  
Draw anything on the canvas and watch the system instantly guess what you’ve created.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Open the game in your browser  
2. Start drawing on the canvas ✏️  
3. The AI analyzes your doodle in real-time 🤖  
4. Get instant predictions of what you drew  
5. Try to beat the AI by drawing tricky sketches 😏  

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Real-Time Prediction**  
  Get instant results as soon as you finish drawing  

- ✏️ **Interactive Canvas**  
  Smooth and responsive drawing experience  

- 🤖 **AI-Powered Recognition**  
  Uses a deep learning model to identify doodles  

- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight**  
  Quick response with minimal delay  

- 🎮 **Fun Gameplay**  
  Challenge yourself or compete with friends
